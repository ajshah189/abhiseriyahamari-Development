# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

AR Airways

A luxury digital companion for the wedding of Abhishek & Riya.

---

# Product Vision

AR Airways transforms a destination wedding into a premium travel experience.

Guests are not simply attendees.

They become passengers travelling around the world.

Every interaction should reinforce this journey.

The website serves as the single digital companion throughout the wedding.
